Customer Satisfaction Project

Predicting customer satisfaction using various parameters to enhance their experience.

Explore Project

Project Description

This project utilizes a logistic regression model to predict customer satisfaction based on various factors:

  • Age: The customer's age.
  • Flight Distance: Distance of the flight in miles.
  • Inflight Entertainment: Rating of inflight entertainment.
  • Baggage Handling: Rating of baggage handling.
  • Cleanliness: Rating of cleanliness.
  • Departure and Arrival Delays: Delay times in minutes.
  • Gender: The gender of the customer.
  • Customer Type: Loyalty status of the customer.
  • Type of Travel: Purpose of travel (personal/business).
  • Class Type: Class of travel (Economy, Economy Plus, Business).
